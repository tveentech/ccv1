5 reasons why you should visit Coffee Castle

Serene

Ours is a fantastic piece of property spread over 7.5 acres of lush green estate. A place cut off from the urban reaches and an ideal place to be one with nature.

Culture

You travel back in time to a simpler era with indigenous people and traditional rural life.

Romantic

Our secluded property near the lake rekindles a lost moment integral to the blossoming of a relationship. Here lovers are blessed with a powerful union of consciousness and nature.

Our lady of the Lake

The lake is very well designed to make it feel as if it is cascading into the forest. Its mesmerizing waters will haunt you forever.

Coffee

One does not talk about Coorg without talking about its coffee. We offer tours around the coffee estate with guides to walk you through the plantation and help you discover its secrets. But the tour is only complete after enjoying frothing saffron flavoured south Indian filter coffee.
